What We Are.

The purpose of Pilot Hub is to give the Client the ability to help its customers make the most of their valuable time during flights by giving the customer luxury & Travel privacy; convenience to hold business and other private meetings; ability to choose take-off time; succinctly the ability to save energy and time, and lessen the stress encountered in using public aircrafts (for instance, not landing closer to your destination, long intermediate stops, etc.)

What We Offer

Customized travel plans

Pilot Hub will work with you to create a travel plan that meets your specific needs and preferences, including your destination, travel dates, and other requirements.

Aircraft selection:

We have access to a variety of aircraft types, from small jets to large, long-range planes. They can help you select the right aircraft for your needs, whether you are traveling solo or with a large group

Flexible scheduling

With us you have more flexibility when it comes to scheduling. You can typically choose departure and arrival times that work best for you, rather than having to adhere to a fixed commercial airline schedule.

Personalized service

Pilot Hub offer personalized service, including on-board amenities, catering, and ground transportation to and from the airport. You can also expect a high level of attention from the flight crew and staff
